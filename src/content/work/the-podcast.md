## The Format

One person. One camera. No guests — at least not yet. The podcast is a founder talking to the future version of his audience. People who want to hear what business actually sounds like when you strip away the performance.

Each episode picks a single idea and goes deep. Not motivational. Not instructional. Just honest thinking from someone who's been in the trenches.

## Why Audio and Video

Writing is where I shape my thinking. But some lessons are better heard than read. The pauses, the emphasis, the moments where I'm searching for the right word — that's where authenticity lives.

The podcast is on YouTube because video creates connection that audio alone can't. You can see when someone means what they're saying.

## What I Talk About

- Why brand positioning is harder than people think
- What running a grocery shop at 19 taught me about human behaviour
- The difference between patience and passivity
- How AI is changing commerce — and what stays the same
- The real cost of building something that lasts

## Where It's Going

The podcast is early. Episode one is live. The goal isn't to become a "content creator." The goal is to build an archive of honest business thinking that compounds over time — just like the brands I build.
